Introducing Fortana

Data you need without a Data plan

Background

I have a smartphone, but unfortunately I had gone over my very small data cap of 50 MB/month already. I was wondering about how I could still find out what a word means or what the weather is like without accessing internet since I didn't have it

The solution

I built a way for you to get the information you need through SMS. I only spent a few hours on it, but at the moment, you can text things such as "Define your-word-here", "Calc 2*2", "What is a virus?", "Who was George Washington?", or "Weather" to 704-350-2797 to find out the information you need.

What's next

Like I said, I only spent a few hours on this app so far. I plan on adding e-mail, Facebook, Twitter, and ESPN integration soon
